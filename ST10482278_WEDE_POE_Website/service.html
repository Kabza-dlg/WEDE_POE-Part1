Our Services

  • Takeaway Orders – Customers can order and collect food quickly.
  • Delivery Service – Free delivery within 5km, small fee beyond.
  • Catering for Events – Traditional meals for weddings, funerals, birthdays, and corporate events.
  • Special Day Menus – Mogodu Mondays, Inhloko Fridays, and more traditional specials.
  • Pre-Order & Bulk Orders – Place large orders in advance for families or groups.
  • Frozen Traditional Foods – Seshebo, chicken feet, giblets, and other frozen packs for home cooking.
  • Meal Combos – Affordable daily combos for students, workers, and families.
  • Community Pop-Up Stalls – Food stands at local markets, events, and soccer matches.
